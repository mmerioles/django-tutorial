Interesting League Facts

Did You Know?

  • League of Legends has over 160 champions, each with unique abilities and playstyles.
  • The first ever World Championship was held in 2011 at DreamHack in Sweden.
  • A professional League match averages around 30-35 minutes in length.
  • The most-watched League of Legends esports event drew over 70 million viewers.
  • Baron Nashor's name is an anagram of "Roshan," the boss from Dota.

Tips to Get Good

  • Master last-hitting: Focus on farming minions efficiently — aim for at least 7 CS per minute to stay ahead in gold.
  • Ward consistently: Vision wins games. Always place control wards and use your trinket on cooldown.
  • Learn the minimap: Glance at the minimap every few seconds to track enemy positions and avoid ganks.
  • Stick to a small champion pool: Playing 2-3 champions well is better than playing 20 champions poorly.
  • Focus on objectives: Towers, dragons, and Baron are how you win — not chasing kills across the map.
  • Review your replays: Watch your deaths and ask what you could have done differently each time.

Gameplay Strategies

  • Wave management: Learn to freeze, slow push, and fast push waves to create advantages in lane.
  • Trading stance: Position near a dying minion to force your opponent to choose between trading and farming.
  • Jungle tracking: Pay attention to where the enemy jungler was last seen and predict their pathing.
  • Play around power spikes: Know when your champion is strongest (level 6, item completion) and fight during those windows.
